Density of states rho(E) per site of the 1D tight-binding chain from routes that agree: solid grey, the closed-form rho=1/(pi sqrt(4t^2-E^2)); dashed green, exact diagonalization of H(k) on a dense k-mesh (on-the-fly, no supercell); open blue circles, exact (dense) diagonalization of the expanded sparse supercell matrix; dash-dotted orange, the kernel polynomial method (M=512 moments, R=30 vectors, Jackson kernel) on that same matrix. The exact curves use a Gaussian broadening eta=0.03 eV. Chain of t=-1, band [-2,2]; the van Hove edges are resolved as the broadening allows.
