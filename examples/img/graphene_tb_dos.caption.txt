Density of states rho(E) per site of the idealized two-band tight-binding graphene (graphene_tb_hr.dat only: nearest-neighbour t=-1, every ndegen=1, no _wsvec.dat -- the minimal tight-binding file set of tutorial 01 on the honeycomb lattice).
